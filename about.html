Our Story

Experience the Graciousness

The long endless paddy fields, the romantic wind, beautiful rubber plantations and the lake-fringed coastal belt welcome you to the alluring land of 'Changanassery'. A land steeped in history and culture, where tradition meets modernity.

Changanassery has acquired a prominent place in Kerala Tourism. Hotel New York Square remains the hallmark of hospitality in this land. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to providing our guests with an unforgettable experience that combines luxury, comfort, and authentic Kerala hospitality.

Each corner of the hotel is crafted with fine decors and luxury designs. Our commitment to excellence shows in every detail - from the carefully curated interiors to our personalized service that makes every guest feel at home.

Our Location

In the Heart of Changanassery

Strategically located in Kottamuri, Trikkodithanam, Hotel New York Square offers easy access to some of Kerala's most beautiful destinations. Whether you're here for business or leisure, you'll find our location perfect for exploring the region.

The serene backwaters, ancient temples, lush plantations, and vibrant local culture are all within easy reach from our hotel. Let us be your gateway to experiencing the true essence of God's Own Country.
