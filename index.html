Unforgettable Authenticity

The long endless paddy fields, the romantic wind, beautiful rubber plantations and the lake-fringed coastal belt welcome you to the alluring land of 'Changanassery'.

Changanassery has acquired a prominent place in Kerala Tourism. Hotel New York Square remains the hallmark of hospitality in this land. Each corner of the hotel is crafted with fine decors and luxury designs. Come; rejoice in nature's cradle.
